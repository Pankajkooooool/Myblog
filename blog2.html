How to hold handstands wayy longer

Step 1: Do it often !

the trick to getting the handstand hold longer is doing more frequently during your day to make your body used to upside down position - it also drastically improves your mind muscle connection. - the frequent exposure to this position also helps in getting stronger shoulders and upperbody overall

Step 2: Master the base..

To hold a proper handstand you have to be able to do atleast 20 pushups perfect form for men and atleast 15 pushups for women After this prerequisite you may proceed to strengthen the movement of hand stand by holding it against the wall for an extended amount of time (ideally for 2 minutes atleast!) This will build the confidence to practice the freestyle handstand. .

Step 3: Perfect the form.

Another key element in holding a handstand is to hold with perfect form. This helps not only in balance but in the long run, will make you reach for longer period of holdss in your handstand If you practice the HS with improper form then everytime you practice holding the H/s it makes it difficult to correct it. its not only unhealthy calesthenics practice but it will take a toll on your muscle and joints in the long run. You want to make sure you get the form right! - Record yourself doing the Proper form - Ask some other person to monitor your form - Always engage all your body muscles to perform handstand.

Step 4: Do it everyday eachday, consistently.

Getting to your handstand hold can be very frustrating, you feel like you've hit a plateau and do not see any improvement, And you wont always be motivated to do it Just remember the price is worth the pain, Just keep doing it because you enjoy the journey of getting there and not the Destination itself You will realize how much this one skill improve your strength & build muscle, your mind-body connection will improve. Calesthenics is a skill that takes time and trust me you will love the result.
